Tom & Jerry

1940

The classic cat-and-mouse chase that defined cartoon slapstick comedy for generations.

  • Created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera
  • Won 7 Academy Awards for Best Animated Short
  • Originally produced for MGM from 1940 to 1958

Pingu

1986

A lovable claymation penguin who speaks in penguin language and gets into mischief in Antarctica.

  • Created by Otmar Gutmann
  • Originally aired on Swiss television
  • No dialogue - just penguin sounds!
  • Beloved by children worldwide for his funny antics

Ninja Turtles

1987

Four mutated turtle brothers trained in ninjutsu who fight crime in New York City.

  • "Cowabunga!" became their catchphrase
  • Each turtle is named after a Renaissance artist
  • Originally a comic book before the cartoon

X-Men

1992

Mutant superheroes fighting for peace and equality in a world that fears and hates them.

  • Based on the Marvel Comics team
  • Iconic theme song
  • Ran for 5 seasons with 76 episodes
  • Inspired teamwork and courage among fans

Mr. Bean

1990

A live-action character whose animated adventures brought his unique brand of physical comedy to kids.

  • Rowan Atkinson voiced the animated version
  • Ran from 2002 to 2019
  • Featured Teddy, his beloved teddy bear

The Pink Panther

1964

The cool, mischievous panther who starred in animated shorts and his own series.

  • Created for the opening credits of the 1963 film
  • Henry Mancini composed the iconic theme
  • Almost never speaks in the cartoons
